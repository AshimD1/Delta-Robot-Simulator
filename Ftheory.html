VIRTUAL ROBOT SIMULATOR
Theory Objective Procedure Simulation




                    Theory:


Delta robot is a type of parallel robot which is increasingly being used in the industry. The robot can control its approach to various applications quickly and smoothly. As a result, moving into various positions is simple. It may travel in different directions and is appropriate for moving workpieces. Up to 500 grams can be used as the payload. It is applicable to the current state of the food manufacturing industry. As a result, the purpose of this study is to show how to visualize a fully functional rotating delta robot.

It includes :


  •   Frame (Base)
  •   Proximal Link (Active Link)
  •   Distal Link(Passive Link)
  •   Joints
  •   Moving Platform (End Effector)
